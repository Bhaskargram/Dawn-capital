📐 Recommended Logo Sizes
Location	Recommended Size	Format
Website header	200×50 px or wider (height ~44–50px)	PNG with transparent background
Website footer	200×50 px	PNG transparent
Sidebar (Admin/Customer)	48×48 px icon or 200×48 px horizontal	PNG transparent
Mobile app (splash)	512×512 px (will scale down)	PNG transparent
Mobile login	256×256 px	PNG transparent
Favicon	32×32 px or 64×64 px	ICO or PNG
S3/CDN upload	1024×1024 px master (highest quality)	PNG transparent
Best practice: Upload your logo as 1024×1024 px PNG with transparent background to S3 and let CSS/styles scale it down. This ensures it's sharp on retina/high-DPI displays. The current dawn6.png and dawn3.png should ideally be at least 500px wide for crisp rendering.

Once you update the MongoDB URI and restart the backend, everything will work. Let me know your MongoDB URI and I can update the .env for you, or you can do it directly.
